# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include respectful communication and constructive feedback.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will review and investigate all complaints fairly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
